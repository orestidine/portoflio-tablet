Career Trophies Won

This table showcases the career trophies Messi has won and the seasons in which he won it. All of the club trophies(UEFA Champions League, La Liga, Copa del Rey, Spanish Supercup, UEFA Super Cup, FIFA Club World Cup) were won with Fc Barcelona. Messi has won one international trophy with Argentina, which is the Copa America in 2021.

Trophy Years Won
UEFA Champions League 2005-2006, 2008-2009, 2010-2011, 2014-2015
La Liga 2004-2005, 2005-2006, 2008-2009, 2009-2010, 2010-2011, 2012-2013, 2014-2015, 2015-2016, 2017-2018, 2018-2019
Copa del Rey 2008-2009, 2011-2012, 2014-2015, 2015-2016, 2016-2017, 2017-2018, 2020-2021
Spanish Supercup 2005-2006, 2006-2007, 2009-2010, 2010-2011, 2011-2012, 2013-2014, 2016-2017, 2018-2019
UEFA Super Cup 2009-2010, 2011-2012, 2015-2016
FIFA Club World Cup 2009-2010, 2011-2012, 2015-2016
International Trophies 2021 Copa America
